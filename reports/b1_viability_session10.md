# Phase B1 — Viability check (Session 10)

Parallel subagent viability checks for 6 candidate brands. Result: 5 brands proceed to B2 Phase 1 research; 1 brand excluded.

## Proceeding to Phase B2 with 5 brands

- chrysler: 3 models
- dodge: 2 models
- fiat: 1 model
- bugatti: 2 models (tourbillon, w16-mistral; Bolide excluded as track-only per project precedent — Aston Martin Valkyrie AMR Pro, Lotus Evija, McLaren W1 also excluded)
- vinfast: 2 models

**Total: 10 models to research in B2.**

## Excluded: Karma

Karma is operational (US HQ + assembly + dealers) but the project requires the manufacturer to be "actively selling 2026 MY" at scale. With 146 cars built in 2024, a target of ~250 in 2025, Revero production ending late 2025, and Gyesera/Kaveya/Amaris/Ivara stuck in pre-production with slipping timelines, Karma does not meet the threshold. The brand is documented here for future reconsideration if Gyesera or Kaveya enters at-scale delivery.
